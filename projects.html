Some of Our Projects

A selection from our portfolio of private residences, corporate spaces, and heritage properties worldwide.

Private Residence

Private Residence, London

A sculptural olive tree commands the living space with panoramic views of the London skyline and The Shard. The natural trunk creates a stunning focal point against the industrial concrete ceiling and warm oak floors.

Private Residence

Manor, Cotswolds

A pair of matching olive trees flank the sweeping staircase of this distinguished country estate. The coffered ceiling, warm stone finishes, and natural light from the garden beyond complement the Mediterranean greenery.

Corporate

Corporate Head Office, Zurich

A commanding centrepiece for a triple-height atrium. The ancient trunk rises from a bronze planter beneath the skylight, creating a natural focal point that welcomes visitors and anchors the reception space.

Private Office

Private Office, London

An elegant olive tree in a polished brass planter graces this executive suite. Set against book-matched marble walls and floor-to-ceiling windows, it brings organic warmth to a space designed for quiet contemplation.

Private Residence

Private Residence, London

Selected for its exceptional character, this ancient trunk features a dramatic gnarled form that holds its own against panoramic city views. The warm wood paneling and brass planter catch the evening light.

Private Residence, Tuscany

Bringing Mediterranean warmth to a historic palazzo. The traditional terracotta vessel harmonises with ornate coffered ceilings, a Murano chandelier, original frescoes, and views through to the courtyard beyond.
