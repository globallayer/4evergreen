The Challenge

Maintaining live plants in luxury spaces presents significant challenges for discerning clients.

Constant Maintenance

Regular watering, pruning, and specialist care requirements

Environmental Demands

Specific sunlight, humidity, and temperature needs

Health Concerns

Allergens, pollen, and indoor air quality issues

Inconsistent Appearance

Seasonal changes and declining health over time

Ongoing Costs

Professional gardeners, replacements, and irrigation systems

Space Limitations

Many luxury spaces lack adequate natural light
